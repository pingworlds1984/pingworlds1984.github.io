This Privacy Policy explains how information about you is collected, used and disclosed and applies to your use of the Ping android client that we provide.

Information Collection

Crash reports, analysis logs

With your consent, we will collect crash reports and analytics logs through Google Systems to help us troubleshoot and locate program errors and improve our service.

Email, nickname, avatar

If you subscribe to our service, we will collect your email address, nickname, and avatar in order to distinguish between user levels and remove advertisements, but if you do not subscribe, we will not collect this information.

Information sharing

We will integrate with google advertising system, please refer to https://policies.google.com/privacy for google privacy policy, other than that, we will not share the collected information to third parties.

Deleting your account

Subscribers can request to delete your account information after unsubscribing by sending an email to pingworlds@gmail.com
